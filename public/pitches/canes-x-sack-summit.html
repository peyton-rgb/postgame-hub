Short-Form Social

Six concepts built to travel.

Social-first ideas built on Cane's-isms — humor, competitiveness, and brand authenticity, aimed squarely at the feed and at the hosts' followings.

Concept 01

Do You Like B.O.B.?

Hosts get hit with "Do you like BOB?" The reveal: Buttered On Both sides — the Texas Toast. The interviewer runs it holding a piece of training toast as the mic. Sorts the true Caniacs from the rest.

Concept 02

The Box Combo Challenge

"Every hour on the hour, how many Box Combos could you put away?" Let the competitive streak of a room full of pros answer — honest, and probably a little unhinged.

Reference · the format
Concept 03

The Magic Wand

"If Todd Graves walked in with a magic wand, what one menu item would you take a lifetime supply of?" A nod to the founder and everyone's desert-island Cane's pick.

Concept 04

The Special Sauce

"What's your 'special sauce' — something your fans don't know about you?" A play on Cane's Sauce that opens into real stories and fun facts, and a deeper connection with the audience.

Concept 05

Beyond "The Obvious"

Rides the "okay, but besides that" trend. The obvious Cane's answer is the chicken fingers — so we take those off the table. "Besides that, what's your go-to?" and the Crinkle-Cut Fries, the Texas Toast, the slaw, and the Sweet Tea finally get their moment. Trend-native and instantly legible on the feed.

Concept 06

Would You Rather?

"Would you rather fight one Von Miller–sized chicken, or a hundred chicken-finger–sized Von Millers?" An outrageous, thought-provoking bit built for big reactions — riffing on the Overtime "would you rather" format.

Reference · the format
Shot horizontal, framed for 9:16 delivery. Talent recorded on hidden wireless lavaliers tucked behind Cane's "training" props — the toast, the chicken fingers — for clean audio amid the field noise.
