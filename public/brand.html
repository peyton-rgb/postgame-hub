05 / Voice & Language

How Postgame Talks

Voice

  • Confident, athlete-forward, no fluff.
  • Talk to brands and athletes like peers — never like an agency pitching them.
  • Short sentences. Active voice.
  • Sports-aware but never corny.

Trademark Rules

  • These NCAA-owned terms never appear in any copy, caption, or asset:
  • ✕"March Madness" → ✓"the Big Dance" / "the tournament"
  • ✕"Final Four" → ✓"the national semifinal"
  • ✕"Elite Eight" → ✓"the regional final"
  • ✕"Sweet Sixteen" → ✓"the round of 16"

Logo Do's

  • ✓Use the files on this page, at provided proportions
  • ✓Give the mark clear space on all sides
  • ✓Place on True Black, Off-White, or photography with contrast

Logo Don'ts

  • ✕Never retype "POSTGAME" in any font as a stand-in for the logo
  • ✕Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects
  • ✕Never place on low-contrast or busy backgrounds
